Free Walking Tours Of Istanbul

Free Tours of Istanbul offers daily guided walking tours, packed with fun facts, anecdotes, and laughter. Dive into the city's rich history, explore the vibrant street art scene with their "Alternative Istanbul" tour, and get acquainted with the captivating old Ottoman capital – all at no cost!

Step inside the Byzantine basilica of Aya Sofya

A marvel of the ancient world, the Aya Sofya basilica now welcomes visitors free of charge following its reconsecration as a mosque in 2020. This architectural masterpiece boasts the once-second-largest dome in the world, built in the 6th century. Sunlight streams through its 40 windows, bathing the opulent mosaics, frescoes, and multicolored marble in an ethereal glow, leaving visitors awestruck by its timeless beauty.

Parks: Yıldız, Sultanahmet, Gulhane, Emirgan

Escape the city's buzz and embrace tranquility at Istanbul's verdant parks! Yıldız Park in Beşiktaş beckons with rolling hills, winding paths, and captivating Bosphorus views, a favorite among locals and tourists. In the heart of the historic center, Sultanahmet Park offers a charming respite with its meticulously maintained lawns and a central fountain that comes alive with color at night.

Explore the hippodrome at Sultanahmet Square

Right next to the iconic Blue Mosque, Sultanahmet Square offers a free history lesson. Step back in time at the Hippodrome of Constantinople, sharing the same space. Marvel at the Serpent's Column, a centerpiece of the track's central barrier. Don't miss the two grand obelisks flanking the Hippodrome: the Obelisk of Theodosius and the Obelisk of Constantine Porphyrogenitus, silent witnesses to the city's rich past.

4 Days Itinerary

Experience Istanbul's magic on a budget! This 4-day itinerary unlocks hidden gems, bustling bazaars, and iconic landmarks for solo travelers and budget-conscious adventurers. Explore vibrant streets, savor street food, and discover a captivating city beyond the usual tourist trail. Embrace the freedom to wander and find your own Istanbul adventure!

  • Day 1

    Exploring Historic Istanbul

    Budget: 43 AED

    Look for free walking tours!

    8:00AM-9:00PM

    Morning: Start your day with a visit to the majestic Hagia Sophia. While there's an entrance fee (57 AED) for the interior, you can admire the stunning exterior for free. Stroll around Sultanahmet Square, soaking in the historical atmosphere.
    Afternoon: Explore the Blue Mosque, another architectural marvel with free entry. Head to the Grand Bazaar, a sensory overload of sights and smells. Browse the shops, but be prepared to bargain!
    Evening: Enjoy a delicious and affordable street food dinner in the Sultanahmet area. Sample gözleme (stuffed flatbread) or döner kebap (around 14-29 AED). Catch a free cultural event like a traditional music performance or a whirling dervish ceremony outside historical sites.

  • Day 2

    Unveiling Hidden Gems

    Budget: 29 AED

    Download offline maps!

    9:00AM-7:00PM

    Morning: Take a ferry across the Bosphorus Strait to the Asian side (around 7 AED) for stunning city views. Explore the charming Üsküdar district with its historical mosques and lively waterfront promenade.
    Afternoon: Wander through the colorful Balat district, known for its restored Ottoman houses and vibrant streets. Get lost exploring the Fener district, the historical center of the Greek Orthodox community.
    Evening: Enjoy a sunset picnic in Gülhane Park, offering free entry and beautiful views. Alternatively, grab a budget-friendly meal at a local pide (Turkish flatbread pizza) restaurant (around 14-29 AED).

  • Day 3

    Spice Up Your Day

    Budget: 43 AED

    Learn a few basic phrases in the local language!

    9:00AM-7:00PM

    Morning: Immerse yourself in the sights and smells of the Egyptian Spice Bazaar. Marvel at the colorful displays of spices, teas, and Turkish delights. Don't forget to haggle for good deals! (Free entry)
    Afternoon: Visit the Yeni Mosque, known for its unique architecture and beautiful courtyard (free entry). Take a walk along the Galata Bridge, a historic landmark with vibrant restaurants and cafes. Enjoy a cup of çay (Turkish tea) for a refreshing break (around 7 AED).
    Evening: Enjoy the lively atmosphere of Taksim Square, the heart of modern Istanbul. Mingle with locals and experience the city's vibrant nightlife scene.

  • Day 4

    Relax and Reflect

    Budget: 29 AED

    Carry some small change for local vendors!

    9:00AM-4:00PM

    Morning: Explore the historic city walls surrounding Istanbul, offering free entry and panoramic views. You can also visit the Suleymaniye Mosque, a grand Ottoman-era mosque with free entry (donations appreciated).
    Afternoon: Relax at a traditional Turkish bath (hammam) for a cultural experience. Many hamams offer separate times for men and women. Prices can vary, but some offer budget-friendly options (around 43-71 AED).
    Evening: Enjoy a farewell dinner at a rooftop bar or cafe with stunning city views (drinks and snacks around 14-29 AED). Watch the sunset and reflect on your Istanbul adventure.
